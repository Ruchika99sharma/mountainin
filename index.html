About us!

Hotel Mountain INN is situated in Manali, 1.5 km from Mall Road, Manu Temple is 3.7 km, Hadimba Devi Temple 3 km, while Solang Valley is 14 km from the property.

The rooms are equipped with a balcony with garden views. All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV. Every room comes with a private bathroom. The hotel provides free WiFi also.

Guests at Hotel Mountain INN can enjoy breakfast and Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which specialises in North & South Indian cuisine, Chinese cuisine. The accommodation offers a children's playground. Bike hire and car hire are available at this hotel.
